ZÔDAHÔ

Strength Meets Speed.

The Zôdahô logo is a visual metaphor for the brand's core promise. Drawing from "Zo" (Elephant) and "Dacho" (Ostrich), it embodies duality. The heavy, 3D font represents power and reliability, while sharp angular cuts symbolize speed and agility. The vibrant orange supercharges the identity with energy.
